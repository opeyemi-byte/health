About Maryland Hospital

Maryland Hospital was established in the year 2012, it was established with the sole purpose of caring for Nigerians and making sure the people of the country have access to effective medical care.

Maryland hospital also provides online services and consultation for clients everywhere, you can contact a doctor and ask them to come to you and they will be there swiftly.

Our Hospital is well equipped with latest medical equipments such as the heartbeat monitor machine, blood pressure machine, x-ray machine amongst others. We also provide the best Laboratory service in the country.

Our CEO with his education and experience has made sure that only qualified and efficient medical personnels attend to all customers. The wellbeing of our patients is No 1 on our minds.

Maryland Hospitals care for people of every age and condition, we have qualified Neurologist, Radiologist,Surgeons,
